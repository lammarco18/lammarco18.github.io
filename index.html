Civil Engineering Projects

Arch Dam Heightening – 3D Numerical & Thermal Analysis

Large arch dam • ~120 m • Dam heightening project • 2023 – ongoing

3D numerical modelling and thermal analysis for the planned heightening of a large arch dam in a mountainous region. The goal is to evaluate the stress state of the raised dam under construction stages and operating conditions, and to compare results against existing reference calculations.

  • 3D FEM static analysis and staged-construction modelling with FLAC3D.
  • Thermal assessment (maximum temperature, gradients, cracking risk) during casting and cooling.
  • Comparison and calibration of numerical models with established design approaches.
  • Use of Python scripting and in-house tools for pre- and post-processing.

FEM Thermal Analysis Static Analysis FLAC3D Python

Seismic Assessment of a Portfolio of Large Dams

Concrete gravity & masonry dams • H up to ~160 m • 2021 – ongoing

Seismic and static safety assessment for a portfolio of large dams located in low seismicity regions. Monitoring data are also analysed to evaluate dam behaviour under different loading conditions.

  • Static and seismic checks with CADAM2D/3D and FLAC2D.
  • Assessment of dam behaviour using monitoring time series.
  • Post-seismic behaviour evaluation and material parameter investigations.
  • Development of Python tools and GUI for data analysis and reporting.

Seismic Analysis Dam Behaviour Monitoring CADAM FLAC2D Python
